What our clients say

A1tutor is an Educational Technology (Edtech) company with a goal of making qualitative and personalised educational content available and accessible to primary, secondary and post-secondary learners across Africa. We are an African-owned and African-focused company with extensive reach in the Nigerian educational sector and rapidly expanding to the West-African region and other parts of Africa. We adopt a technology-driven, student-centred approach to learning; through tailored educational content that is easily available in various formats and across multiple platforms.

We provide concise lesson notes, interactive instructional videos, topic-related quizzes, Online/Offline CBT (Mock) tests, and a proprietary Tutor Live Chat feature for learners in Primary School (Class 1-6), Junior Secondary School (JSS 1-3), Senior Secondary School (SSS 1-3) and A-levels (IJMB & JUPEB). We also offer examination preparatory content for learners taking different categories of examinations including NCEE (Common Entrance), BECE (Junior WAEC), WASSCE, UTME, IJMB and JUPEB candidates.

Our educational content is well-researched, carefully curated and expertly delivered by seasoned tutors across several specialisations. Our content is prepared in line with the approved educational curriculum for learners by examination bodies such as NCEE (Common Entrance), BECE, NECO, WAEC, JAMB, IJMB & JUPEB.

We offer educational content in text, audio, video, audio-visual and infographic formats. Our multi-format approach allows us to use the most appropriate medium to present educational content making learning memorable and fun for our users. Users can access our content through their Web browser or the A1tutor app (available on both Google Play and App Store).

KEY FEATURES

A1tutor’s Unique Value Proposition

Student-centred approach

Our students are the centre of our learning approach. We believe that learning should be student-focused at all times. Students have different patterns of learning that are not often catered for in group classes. This is why we provide personalised learning options so learners can use their preferred content format, learn at their own pace and customise their learning experience all the way. Our expert educational consultants are trained and available to guide at all times.

Comprehensive Curriculum

The A1tutor platform offers an expansive array of educational content covering several curriculums across all levels of education. Subjects ranging from Mathematics, English Language, Biology, Literature-in-English, Business Studies, Basic Science, Financial Accounting, Chemistry, Government, Economics, Physics etc. are all available. We offer the most in-depth and comprehensive curriculum catering for all categories of learners

Community Learning

Learning on the internet can be fun, interactive and mutually beneficial. A1tutor provides subject-specific forums for all subjects so learners can leverage the strength of their community to accelerate their learning through group discussions, question & answer sessions and group tests. Premium Users.

Proprietary Tutor Live Chat

We have a developed an industry-leading Tutor Live Chat feature that connects learners with tutors in real-time. Our Tutor Live Chat is manned by subject-matter experts who provide answers to our learners’ questions, making their learning experience more immersive. The Tutor Live Chat is available round-the-clock to our Premium users.
