Our Products & Services

We are a full-service Educational Technology (Edtech) company with a bouquet of services for personal (single) users, corporate organisations and educational institutions. Our services are explained below

Custom lesson notes & videos

At the centre of our service offering is our custom-made lesson notes & interactive instructional videos for all subjects and across all educational levels. Learners can access our carefully curated lesson notes and instructional videos on-the-go through their devices. All our content formats are optimised for both mobile and web responsiveness. Click here to access lesson notes and videos

Examination Preparation

We recognise that the joy of studying is in acquired knowledge and examination success. Thus, beyond providing educational content, we provide examination preparation services to learners for different examinations. Our examination preparation coverage includes NCEE (Common Entrance), BECE (Junior WAEC), WASSCE, NECO, GCE, UTME, IJMB, JUPEB. We have recorded over a 90% success rate from our examination preparation services. Click here to sign up for the Examination Preparation Plan.

Online/Offline CBT (Mock) Tests

We provide a simulated examination experience for our users through the CBT (Mock) Test platforms. Learners can prepare for various CBT examinations and practice with over 20,000 past questions on the platform. Premium users can also practise CBT (Mock) Tests with their friends through the ‘Practice CBT Online with Friends’ feature. Click here to take a CBT (Mock) Test

Institutional Access

We provide custom-made institutional access for schools (primary & secondary), other educational institutions (UTME Training centres, A-level centres etc.), and other institutions (clubs & societies, PTAs, religious institutions etc.). Institutions can sign-up multiple learners at a discounted rate. Click here to discuss with our Business Development Manager for a custom package

Student Guidance

Our certified learner liaison officers provide person-specific educational guidance and advice to all learners. We will guide you through your learner-experience journey and show you how to get the best out of the A1tutor platform. Click here to book a free student guidance session

User/IT Support

Our dedicated team of IT specialists are available to help learners and/or their guardians with all IT-related questions on both the web and app platforms. Click here to contact our IT Support or click here to download our IT guide
